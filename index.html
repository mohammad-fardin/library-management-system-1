Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow books from the library?

You can browse our catalog online and reserve books using your library account. Once reserved, you can pick them up at the library.

Can I renew books online?

Yes, you can renew borrowed books through your online account unless another user has reserved the same book.

Are there late fees for overdue books?

Yes, late fees are charged for overdue books. Please check our policies section for more details on fees.

How do I access e-books?

We provide access to a wide range of e-books. Log in to your account, browse the e-book section, and download them to your device.
